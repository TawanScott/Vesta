Vesta

Earth is the cradle of humanity, but together we will open space for all Mankind.

Ambition

At Vesta, we recognize that throughout history, nothing has challenged mankind more than space exploration. Space has expanded our understanding of science as well as human ingenuity, physiology, and psychology. It is our desire to continue to push these boundaries and achieve what we believed is the pinnacle of humanity's achievements.

Pursuit

Commercial spaceline has never been accomplished despite the fact that millions of people would love to experience space. As the world's first commercially operational aerospace company, it is our mission to deliver unparalleled spaceflights to our customers.

explore our pursuit

Aspiration

Every new industry has a foundation and we made it our passion to be that foundation for the space tourism industry through commercial viability and rapid innovation. In time, the economies of scale and competing technologies will create downward pressure on the cost of launch, allowing more users to be able to experience the sea of space.

Fly with us

These are the spacecraft that will take you to space. They are the most innovative feats of engineering in this industry that have been designed to maximize comfort, astronaut experience, and most importantly, safety.

Avalon

Avalon is our largest spacecraft with room for 2500 passengers. It was built with a vision of being able to provide a space exploration experience for a reduced cost of launch per customer compared to our other spacecraft.

Learn more

Hyperion

Hyperion can accommodate 1000 passengers and it was designed as the middle ground between the Avalon and Odyssey, which will provide our customers with a lower cost of launch while slightly increasing passenger's space compared to Avalon.

Learn more

Odyssey

Odyssey is our medium-sized spacecraft that can accommodate 300 passengers. Customers will feel familiar with the Odyssey due to the compact size that it provides, which is similar to normal-sized commercial aircraft.

Learn more

Icarus

Icarus is our most private and exclusive spacecraft that can accommodate eight passengers (including two pilots). This spacecraft was designed for the most luxurious group of adventurers in the world who are ready to join us for a world-class astronaut experience.
